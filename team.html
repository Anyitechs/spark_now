Dana Delvin

Dana Devlin is the founder and CEO of Spark Now. She holds a BS in Psychology from the University of Central Florida. Throughout her career, Dana has held several positions across various industries including Real Estate, Fitness, and Education. Through it all, she still found the time to start her first tech start-up! The idea of building the Spark Now app came to her when she had a disagreement with her husband about vacuuming! Dana currently lives with her husband Jay in Orlando Florida and they have two pets. In her spare time, she enjoys going to the gym and spending time with her family.
